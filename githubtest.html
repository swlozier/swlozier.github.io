Digital Humanities Sequence Changes

Until Fall 2023, the Upper-Division DCD Digital Humanities Sequence was made up of the following 5 classes: ENGL 231: Film, New Media, and Culture; DCD 309: Interactivity and Culture; DCD 316: Digital Resources in the Humanities; DCD 345: Knowledge Production and Digital Representation; and (choose 1 of 2) DCD 312: Social Media or COMM 451: Interactive Media and Society. Following an evaluation of the program I proposed the following changes to the DCD committee:

  • Replace DCD 345: Knowledge Production and Digital Representation with DCD 300: Special Topics in Digital Studies

These changes were approved by the English department in February 2023, and brought to academic affairs for inclusion in the 2023-2024 catalog in March 2023.

As of Fall 2023, the five-course DH Sequence is ENGL 231: Film, New Media, and Culture; DCD 300: Special Topics in Digital Studies; DCD 309: Interactivity and Culture; DCD 316: Digital Resources in the Humanities; and (choose 1 of 2) DCD 312: Social Media or COMM 451: Interactive Media and Society.

Group 2 Methods Sequence Changes 

Until Fall 2023, the Group 2 Methods Sequence was made up of the following classes from across the Edwards College, from which students are required to choose 2: GEOG 310: Digital Cartography, GEOG 311: Earth Observation, HFA 391: Press Project Workshop, HIST 396: Manuscripts and Archives, An Introduction, HIST 397: Digital History, JOUR 304: Print and Online News Writing, JOUR 305: Journalism News Writing and Reporting for Media (Print and Online), JOUR 314: Broadcast Newsand Sports Television Reporting, MCJ 391: Recording Technology I, MCJ 392: Recording Technology II, POLI 305: Introduction to Empirical Political Inquiry, and THEA 255: Computer Aided Drafting and Design.Following an evaluation of the program and consultation with Sue Bergeron in Geography, I proposed the following changes to the DCD committee

  • Remove GEOG 311: Earth Observation from the list;
  • Add GEOG 456: Video Game Worlds; GEOG 450: Digital Heritage, Historical Digital Reconstruction; and GEOG 452: Digital Heritage, Virtual Landscapes

These changes were approved by the English department in February 2023, and brought to academic affairs for inclusion in the 2023-2024 catalog in March 2023.

As of Fall 2023, the Group 2 Methods options are (choose 2) GEOG 310: Digital Cartography, GEOG 456: Video Game Worlds, GEOG 450: Digital Heritage, Historical Digital Reconstruction, GEOG 452: Digital Heritage, Virtual Landscapes, HFA 391: Press Project Workshop, HIST 396: Manuscripts and Archives, An Introduction, HIST 397: Digital History, JOUR 304: Print and Online News Writing, JOUR 305: Journalism News Writing and Reporting for Media (Print and Online), JOUR 314: Broadcast News and Sports Television Reporting, MCJ 391: Recording Technology I, MCJ 392: Recording Technology II, POLI 305: Introduction to Empirical Political Inquiry, and THEA 255: Computer Aided Drafting and Design.
