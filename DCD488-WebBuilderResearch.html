Web Builders

Each of you will research one platform or program that could be used to build and host your portfolios. When we next meet on Monday, Feb 6, you give a short presentation on the platform you researched. You do not have to prepare a formal presentation unless you find this helpful, but your presentation must include the following information:
  • How much does it cost to use this program or platforms at different levels?
  • What kinds of options are there for styling? Does this platform use themes? Do the themes incur addition cost?
  • How much custom coding do you have to do? How much CAN you do? 
  • How does the builder handle web hosting? Can you get a custom URL? How much more does this cost? 
  • Anything else of interest?
