People and Their Social Media Cultures

General Prompt

For your third major unit assignment, you will continue thinking through the people of social media, and the ways that people engage with, interact with, and work on these platforms.As we have discussed over the course of this unit, there is a wide range of ways that people work for and engage with these platforms, which includes content moderation to influencers.

Topical Areas to Explore

Content Moderation Policies and Practices: For this topic, you will explore and area that we opened the unit with, but have otherwise not spent much class time on: content moderation. On a platform of your choice, conduct a deep-dive research into their content moderation policy and practice -- what do their community guidelines say about their content moderation? What does their company culture say? What kinds of journalistic or academic reports can you find about their content moderation? What do users have to say about their content moderation? Are the policies applied equally across the platform? Do you agree with their content moderation policy and practice? Why/not?

Comparative Study of Influencers:For this topic, you will continue exploring an area we've spent a lot of time on this unit: influencers. While this may begin with your work from the influencer show and tell exercise, you should expand that work so that this assignment will be a comparative analysis. There are two ways you can do your comparative analysis: for one, limit your research to one platform and compare three types of influencers on that platform (eg: a lifestyle influencer, a healthcare influencer, and food influencer), looking at the various ways the use the platform to exert their influence, the ways strategies may change depending on the sub-area, and looking to develop an informational essay about influencing practices on this platform. The second option is to look at one influencer and compare their practices across platforms, noting ways their instagram strategy is consistent with and different from their tiktok strategy (for instance). For this option, you will look to produce an informative report on one influencer's practices, offering this limited close evaluation and study as a lens through which to understand influencing more broadly.

Analytical Report of a Social Media subculture: This option invites you to explore an area we have not spent much class time on beyond readings: deep-diving and reporting on a subculture from social media, using the articles about food culture and urban / aesthetic culture post-Instagram as guides. For this topic, you will select a social media subculture of your choice -- GRWM, make-up tutorials, haul culture, books, gaming, food, art, etc -- and do a deep dive into this subculture as represented on a platform of your choice, then offer an analytical report on what this culture is, what its parameters and practices are, and how on- and offline cultures are influenced or responsive to it.

Once you have conducted your research, report your findings and insights, following either the critical or creative guidelines below. (Keep in mind, the creative platform for this unit is Instagram).

Creative Option

Per the syllabus, with each major assignment you have the option to do a more traditional critical written piece or a more creative multimedia piece, where you work in formats appropriate to the given unit's platform focus.

For this unit, our platform focus is Instagram so your creative option will be to use the Instagram platform, its affordances, and expectations as a creative outlet for your work. In other words, you will constrain your piece to what is expected, allowed, and appropriate for Instagram, while focusing on the same topical areas described above. Designer's Statement

If you choose to do the creative option, you will also be responsible for submitting a written designer's statement, wherein you articulate and reflect on your creative process and goals.

Your designer's statements should be 500-750 words and address the following:

  • What were your goals for creatively using Instagram to communicate your research findings on the topical area of your choice?
  • How does your creative project use the affordances of Instagram to share your research findings?
  • Where did you struggle in this project? How did you overcome these struggles?
